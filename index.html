MultiCalendar Sync solves the challenge of managing availability across multiple company calendars. When you have appointments in one company's calendar, this add-in ensures your busy time is reflected in all other connected calendars, so colleagues from different companies can see your accurate availability.

Key Features:

  • Cross-Calendar Sync: Syncs busy time across up to 5 different calendars
  • Privacy Protected: Only shows busy/free time, not appointment details
  • Selective Control: Choose which calendars to include in synchronization
  • Real-time Updates: Automatic sync when appointments change
  • Clean Interface: Integrated seamlessly into Outlook

How to Use:
