Privacy Policy - MultiCalendar Sync

Last updated: August 11, 2025

Overview

MultiCalendar Sync is designed with privacy as a core principle. This add-in helps synchronize busy time across multiple calendars while maintaining the highest standards of data protection.

Data We Access

  • Calendar busy/free status: We only access whether you are busy or free during specific time periods
  • Calendar names: We access the names of your calendars to display them in the interface
  • Appointment times: We access start and end times of appointments for synchronization

Data We DO NOT Access

  • Meeting subjects or titles
  • Meeting attendees
  • Meeting locations
  • Meeting content or descriptions
  • Personal information beyond calendar data

Data Storage

  • Local Storage Only: All settings and preferences are stored locally in your browser
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store any data on external servers
  • No Data Transmission: Calendar data is processed entirely within your Outlook client

Data Usage

Calendar data is used exclusively for the purpose of creating busy blocks in your other calendars. No data is used for analytics, marketing, or any other purpose.

Third-Party Services

This add-in only communicates with Microsoft Graph API through your existing Outlook client. No third-party services have access to your data.

Your Rights

  • You can disable the add-in at any time
  • You can choose which calendars participate in synchronization
  • You can delete all local settings by clearing your browser data

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact your organization's IT administrator.
